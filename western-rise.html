Western Rise

A tribute to the wandering soul of man; clothing and accessories designed to compliment the adventure in all of us.

Will is an avid fly-fisherman and outdoorsman, passionate about building a brand around fashionable, functional outdoor gear and accessories. Timeless and quality-made wilderness gear is the idea behind Western Rise. We collaborated with Will to create an identity and voice that matched his vision; something that would scale with their products and age with grace.
