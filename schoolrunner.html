Schoolrunner

Schoolrunner was founded with the vision of empowering educators with instant access to visual data and detailed information on all their students.

Their product is a breath of fresh air in the marketplace for administrative software in the educational world. Teachers intuitively track grades, topic proficiency, behavior, and attendance with one simple application. We worked with them to create a brand identity and product strategy that would capture a whimsical, friendly, and a innovative approach to data and education.
